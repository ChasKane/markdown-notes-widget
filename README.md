# Markdown Notes Widget

Android widget for displaying and editing markdown/text files directly on your home screen. Perfect for Obsidian users and anyone who wants file-based note management.

- **Obsidian users**: Display your vault notes on your home screen

- **File-based workflows**: Edit notes across devices and apps
